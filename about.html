What is a Library Management System ?


 It's a software were basic operations of a library are performed. It,  provides information about the books which are present in the  library. It, also helps to keep a track of books published, given in  return, etc.


 According to the mode of services rendered to the readers  the libraries are broadly divided into four types :
 1 Academic Library.
 2 Special Library.
 3 Public Library.
 4 National Library.
